Law of Conservation

"Matter cannot be created or destroyed."

If you burn a 5kg log, the ash + smoke + gas = 5kg.

Did you know? In a closed system, the mass at the start equals the mass at the end, even after a massive explosion!

Separation Techniques

Mixtures are not chemically bonded, so we can pull them apart!

Heterogeneous Mixture

"Hetero" = Different. You can see the parts.

Pizza Trail Mix Sand & Water

Homogeneous Mixture

"Homo" = Same. Looks uniform throughout (Solution).

Salt Water Air Brass

What it separates: Large solids from small solids.

Uses a mesh screen. Smaller particles fall through, larger ones stay trapped. Like separating rocks from sand at the beach!

What it separates: Insoluble solids from liquids.

Uses filter paper. The liquid (filtrate) passes through tiny pores, but the solid (residue) gets stuck. Like coffee grounds!

What it separates: Dissolved solids from liquids.

Uses heat. The liquid turns to gas (vapor) and floats away, leaving the solid crystals behind. This is how we get sea salt.

What it separates: Magnetic solids from non-magnetic solids.

Magnets pull out iron, nickel, or cobalt. Great for removing iron filings from sand!
